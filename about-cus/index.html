About us

OUR STORY STARTS WITH YOU

Refr is a first-to-the-world product that aims to revolutionize the way merchants connect with their customers. As the world of commerce is evolving immensely, Refr was established to give every business owner an easy way for marketing. The idea of Refr was born as an adjunctive marketing tool in the mind of our founder, a small business owner who has tried various avenues of marketing over a span of decade.

Our mission is to build an open, easy and inclusive ecosystem that benefits all. This lies at the core of our philosophy and business success. We’re building a business that is big, meaningful and lasting by empowering sellers around the world.

Refr is a unique marketing platform that digitalizes Word of Mouth Marketing, enabling businesses to go from offline to online to social on a single platform. With Refr, businesses can effectively leverage their customer’s social network to increase trust and sales, cultivate loyal customer advocates, engage shoppers on our marketplace and much more. Refr helps businesses accelerate their growth by enabling advocacy and maximizing customer lifetime value.

We provide a platform for people to connect with their community and easily recommend businesses to their circle of people. Each recommendation or referral the user makes is appreciated and rewarded in real-time by the business.
